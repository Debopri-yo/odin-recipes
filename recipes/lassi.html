Lassi

Description

Lassi is a refreshing traditional Indian yogurt-based drink, popular for its cooling properties. Made by blending thick yogurt with water or milk, lassi can be served either sweet or salty. Sweet lassi is flavored with sugar, cardamom, and sometimes a touch of rose water or saffron, while salty lassi is seasoned with salt, cumin, and mint.

This creamy beverage is often garnished with chopped nuts, such as pistachios or almonds, and is commonly enjoyed chilled. Lassi is perfect as a cooling summer drink or as an accompaniment to spicy meals.

Ingredients

For Sweet Lassi

  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cold water or milk (optional, for a thinner consistency)
  • 2-3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1-2 teaspoons rose water (optional, for flavor)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for serving)

For Salty Lassi

  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cold water or milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for serving)

Optional Garnishes

  • Chopped pistachios or almonds
  • A few saffron strands (for sweet lassi)
  • Fresh mint leaves (for salty lassi)
